About Me

My name is Vella. I am a 23 year old who is currently residing with my dad and granny in Arizona.


I grew up in a small town in Virgina with my dad and my granny.
Growing up in a small town made me realize how toxic it is when everybody knows everything about everybody.
That is probably the thing I hated the most about my small town.
But I didn't fully hate the town I grew up in.

Growing up in a small town made me realize how nice being away from everything was. You could see the stars at night, without worrying about
all of the city lights being in the way. We didn't have many activites we could do during our free time,
so most of the time, we went for a drive or even to the local Walmart.
In fact, after my Junior Prom, my friends and I decided to go walk around Walmart in our Prom dresses.

I never thought I would move to the other side of the country.





When I found out we were moving in September of 2019, I was nervous, to say the least. I didn't want to leave my friends. I enjoyed my job I had. I didn't
think I would ever be happy in a different state away from the majority of people I knew. I never would've guessed where it led me.
When we moved, I rode with my dad for a bit. He was an OTR truck driver, so we went all around the US. I ended up visiting 46 of the 50 US states.
In May of 2020, we decided to come back to our new home and stay there. 3 years later and now I am working at Amazon in Prep. I'm in the Associate Safety Committee, Glamazon,
Women @ Amazon, FUN Committee, and more. I've worked in almost every department at my facility and am becoming a Learning Ambassador.

Now I am learning Software Development to possibly go a different direction in my career.
